About Me

My name is Fernando Medina, I am originally from Passaic, NJ. I currently live in Lyndhurst, NJ and have been living here for the past year and a half. After high school and some college I was asked to work a couple days on a TV show called Person of Interest it is a show on CBS. I loved working in the film industry and continued that for about 6-7 years. For those that don't know working in film is a lot of hard work and long hours. To get away from such physical work and long hours I decided to take on coding! Joining the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp has been my first step into starting my journey.

Some of my hobbies include longboarding and watching my favorite TV shows with my girlfriend which include Shark Tank, WestWorld, and This is Us. I have a pet tortoise named Shredder aka Lil' Shred aka Bauer (for Jack Bauer). Oh and of course I can't forget my new favorite hobby which is CODING!
